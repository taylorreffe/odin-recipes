Lasagna

About This Recipe

This is my grandmother's favorite lasagna. I used to make it when it got cold every winter and now i hope it can bring some warmth into your home

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Noodles
  • Vegetables

Instructions

  1. Put sauce on bottom of pan
  2. Add layer of noodles
  3. Add layer of vegetables
  4. Add layer of sauce
  5. Repeat, ending on noodle layer
